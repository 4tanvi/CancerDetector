Cancer Prevention and Cures

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain a healthy diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Engage in regular physical activity and maintain a healthy weight.
  • Avoid tobacco use and limit alcohol consumption.
  • Protect your skin from the sun by wearing sunscreen and protective clothing.
  • Get regular screenings and vaccinations as recommended by your healthcare provider.

Cancer Cures

  • Surgery: Removal of the tumor and surrounding tissue.
  • Chemotherapy: Use of drugs to kill cancer cells.
  • Radiation Therapy: Using high doses of radiation to kill cancer cells.
  • Immunotherapy: Helping your immune system fight cancer.
  • Targeted Therapy: Targeting specific molecules involved in cancer growth.
